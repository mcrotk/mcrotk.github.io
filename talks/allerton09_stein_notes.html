M.C. Rotkowitz
On Stein's Paradox and Decentralized Control
Allerton Conference on Communication, Control, and Computing, September 2009.
abstract

Abstract

This talk discusses the implications of Stein's Paradox for control theory, and for decentralized control in particular. We show how a decoupled plant and a seemingly decoupled cost can induce coupling in the optimal controller, as well as unexpected nonlinearity.
